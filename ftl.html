Full Truck Load (FTL)

Dedicated transportation solutions for bulk industrial consignments and large glassware shipments.

Dedicated Capacity

Exclusive Use & Direct Transit

Full Truck Load (FTL) services assign our entire container vehicle exclusively to your cargo. This is the optimal shipping model for large-scale manufacturers, factory wholesale dispatches, and exporters loading high-volume fragile items.

By choosing FTL, you bypass consolidation docks completely. The Eicher Pro 3018 container (19-foot flatbed/box container capacity) loads directly at your facility and drives straight to the destination point. This guarantees the fastest transit speeds and eliminates handling risk.

Fleet Vehicle Specifications

  • ModelEicher Pro 3018 (BS-VI)
  • Container Size19 Feet length x 7.5 Feet width
  • Payload CapacityUp to 12 Metric Tons
Our Method

The FTL Booking & Transit Flow

We follow a strict, owner-operated schedule to ensure seamless bulk shipping.

1

Dispatch Booking

We lock in your shipping dates and dispatch the Eicher Pro 3018 truck directly to your factory or warehouse loading dock in Firozabad.

2

Sealed Loading

Cargo is loaded, strapped securely, and sealed. Once the door is closed, it remains locked and untransferred throughout the entire interstate highway haul.

3

Direct Discharge

Our truck reaches the destination city directly. GPS coordinates are shared with your distributor, ensuring safe, supervised off-loading.
