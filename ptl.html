Part Truck Load (PTL)

Cost-efficient shared shipping space. Pay only for the volume your cargo occupies along our main corridors.

Shared Logistics

Pay Only For What You Ship

For small businesses, retailers, and traders shipping partial consignments, booking a full truck is not cost-effective. Part Truck Load (PTL) solves this by allowing multiple shipments to share our 19-foot container container volume.

We consolidate freight traveling along the same corridor (such as Patna, Bhubaneswar, or Vijayawada). Your cargo is securely partitioned inside our Eicher truck, preventing contact with other shipments. This offers FTL security at a fraction of the cost.

Our Method

The PTL Consolidation Cycle

We optimize shared space through coordinated dispatch cycles along our routes.

1

Cargo Pickup

We pick up your consignment from your Firozabad facility and transport it to our local consolidation center for volumetric measurement.

2

Consolidation Loading

Consignments are sorted by route. Cargo is packed securely into dedicated partitions inside the Eicher Pro 3018 container and locked.

3

Corridor Delivery

The truck completes deliveries along the route. Each partition is unloaded under strict supervision to guarantee no mix-ups.
