About Us

Our Journey

Firozabad's Single-Truck Family Operator

Sawariya Seth Logistics began with a simple mission: to provide the glass manufacturers of Firozabad with a freight partner they could directly hold accountable. As an owner-operated transport service, we manage a dedicated, high-performance Eicher Pro 3018 commercial truck, specializing in fragile glass exports and heavy-duty cargo.

Unlike massive brokerage networks that delegate your cargo to third-party sub-contractors, we operate our own vehicle. When you book with us, you speak directly with the owner who drives the business. This hands-on accountability translates into a 99.8% safe, breakage-free delivery rate.
